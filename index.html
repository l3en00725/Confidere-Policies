Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 7, 2025

Confidere (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our services, including our Meta integrations.

Information We Collect

  • Limited account and advertising data provided via the Meta Marketing API (e.g., ad campaigns, ad sets, insights).
  • Basic contact information that you voluntarily provide (such as your email address).

How We Use Information

  • To provide reporting and management tools for advertising accounts.
  • To improve our internal business services.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data as required by law or to operate our services (e.g., securely storing tokens to enable access to your advertising data).

Data Retention & Deletion

We store tokens and advertising data only as long as needed to provide the service. If you wish to delete your data, contact us at ben@confidere.biz. Upon request, all stored data will be permanently deleted from our systems.

Permissions Disclosure

Our app uses the following Meta permissions:

  • ads_management – to manage campaigns.
  • ads_read – to read ad performance data.
  • read_insights – to view reporting insights.
